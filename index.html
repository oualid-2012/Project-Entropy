Common Questions

FAQ

Is Project Entropy free to play?
Yes — Project Entropy is free to download and play on all platforms. Cosmetic items are available optionally, but core gameplay and all factions are fully accessible at no cost.
Which platforms are supported?
PC (Windows/Mac), iOS and Android. Cross-platform play is supported — a mobile commander can fight alongside a PC ally in real-time.
Is there a single-player campaign?
Yes. An extensive solo campaign follows your test commander through the galactic conflict, with branching story decisions that affect the universe's fate.
How many players per battle?
PvP battles support large-scale multiplayer conflicts with dozens of commanders fighting simultaneously across persistent galaxy maps. Alliance wars involve hundreds.
Can I switch factions?
Each account can run one primary faction, but you may create multiple profiles. Hero recruitment allows you to field units from allied species within your army.
Is it available in my region?
Project Entropy is globally available with full localization for EN, DE, FR, ES, IT, PL, HU, RU, UK, and more. Nordic, Oceanic and South American servers are live.
